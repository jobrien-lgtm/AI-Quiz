What's Your AI
Personality at Work?

AI is reshaping how we work, but not everyone is adapting the same way. Some people are reimagining their entire jobs. Others are finding clever shortcuts. Some are skeptical bystanders, while others are drowning in their own productivity.

There's no single "right" way to use AI — but there are definitely patterns. And pitfalls. Answer honestly about how you're actually using AI at work, not how you think you're supposed to.

Watch out for

Mixed results?

That's actually the most common outcome. Most of us shift between archetypes depending on the task, the pressure we're under, or how much coffee we've had. The point isn't to find your "true type," but to notice your patterns and make more intentional choices about when and how you use AI.
